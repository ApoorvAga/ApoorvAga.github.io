Magic Masters

This was the first major 3D game I made.
In the game, you control spiky who shoots spikes until your fingers touch.
If spiky is hit by the balls he dies instantly.

Each ball has a size and numbers to it. If the number goes the 0, the ball splits in two with their own numbers until a certain size after which it dies.

Each spike which hits the ball counts towards the score. The game keeps getting harder the longer you survive.

Powerups

The game had 3 types of powerups

  • - The Green Coin which inceases the power of the spikes permanently.
  • - The Gold Coin which doubles the number of spikes shot by spiky.
  • - The Blue Coin which stops all the balls at their places so you can kill them better.
