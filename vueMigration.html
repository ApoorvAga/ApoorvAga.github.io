Vue Migration

This was one of my first projects at Provakil.

As Backbone was getting outdated, the company wanted to switch to Vue.js, as Vue is one of the best front-end frameworks today.

I migrated over 10 major components to Vue.
The components were quite complicated. They included extensive filters, Vuex store and lots of frequently changing data.

I worked on this migration for about 4 months as an intern at Provakil.
